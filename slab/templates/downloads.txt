<h2>Running Panopset Applications</h2>
<p>
Substitute flywheel, with the <a href="https://panopset.com">Panopset</a> application name of your choice.
Here are some of the
default paths to the Panopset Flywheel application:
</p>

<h2>Linux</h2>
<pre>
/opt/panopset/bin/flywheel
</pre>
<h2>Macintosh</h2>
<pre>
/Applications/panopset.app/Contents/MacOS/flywheel
</pre>
<h2>Windows</h2>
<pre>
&quot;c:\Program Files\panopset\flywheel.exe&quot;
</pre>
