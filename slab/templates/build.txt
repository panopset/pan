<h1>Build</h1>
<p>
You may build a platform installer from the <a href="https://github.com/panopset/pan">source code</a>.
Run
</p>
<ul><li>
 <b>./bal.sh</b> on Linux Mint (or similar should work) X64 64bit systems, or
</li><li>
 <b>./bam.sh</b> on Apple Macintosh x86 64 bit, or
</li><li>
 <b>baw.cmd</b> on Microsoft Windows x86 64 bit systems.
</li></ul>
<p>
That will put an installer in the src project target directory.
You may need to modify the above scripts for your system. As long as JavaFX applications can be built on your system,
that should just involve modifying the app&lt;Platform&gt;.properties file.
</p>
<p>
Installers for the above listed systems are available on the <a href="/downloads.html">downloads</a> page.
</p>
